Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish mostly prepared from scratch on a Sunday. It can be challenging to make at first but very tasty, and can be done on Sundays as per tradition.

Ingredients

  • Fresh pasta
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Greated Parmesan
  • Basil leaves
  • Mozzarella
  • Besciamella

Steps

  1. Assuming you have made in advance the besciamella, the fresh pasta and the bolognese sauce,
  2. Take an oven dish and put a thin layer of besciamella at the bottom.
  3. lay 2 pasta sheets
  4. put bolognese sauce followed by
  5. greated parmesan, Mozzarella and basil leaves
  6. repeat the above steps until you reach the top of the dish
  7. cover the top with besciamella and more parmesan
  8. put in preheated oven at 180degree C
  9. bake for 25 minutes
  10. Or once crispy and golden
  11. let cool down slightly before serving
