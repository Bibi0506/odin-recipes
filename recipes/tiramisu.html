Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic italian desserts made with coffee and mascarpone cheese

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Eggs
  • Coffee
  • Savoiardi
  • Sugar
  • Liquor

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs with the sugar until it doubles its volume
  2. Add mascarpone cheese mixing carefully with a spatula
  3. Once ready set aside and brew the coffee
  4. add liquor to the coffe and mix
  5. Take the savoiardi biscotti and submerge them for 3 secs in the coffee
  6. lay the biscotti in a dish and then cover them up with the mascarpone cream
  7. Do another layer of biscotti
  8. Cover up with the cream
  9. Store in fridge for at least 4 hours before serving
