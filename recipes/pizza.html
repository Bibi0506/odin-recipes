Pizza

Description

Pizza is another italian dish loved all over the world.

can chose the toppings you prefer, but in this recipe we will make a Margherita. The classic of all

Ingredients

  • Lukewarm water
  • yeast
  • All purpose flour
  • salt
  • EVO oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • mozzarella
  • basil leaves
  1. In a large bowl mix the water with yeast
  2. then add flour and EVO oil and mix until absorbed
  3. add salt and keep kneading
  4. Once the dough is formed and doesn't stick to your hands, is ready
  5. Put it in a bowl and cover to let it rise at least twice its size
  6. Once ready, take a small amount and press it down and round it until you have something resemblign a pizza
  7. Put the tomato sauce and the mozzarella
  8. bake in preheated oven at 200C for 10 minutes or until golden and crispy
